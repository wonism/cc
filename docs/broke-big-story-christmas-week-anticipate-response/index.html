Category : Announcements
Date : 2019-01-02T07:58:24

Dear readers,

Happy 2019! I hope the past year has been great for you, and I look forward to another year of serving you even more insightful content.

2018 certainly felt long for me. A lot happened in the world and at Tech in Asia. It’s why I’m grateful for the holidays – it’s good to slow down, reflect, and catch up on some books (psst scroll down to see what I’ve been reading).

But even as we were preparing for office closure, our dashboard lit up like a Christmas tree. People were flocking to this story: Shopee overtakes Lazada and Tokopedia to claim Southeast Asia’s ecommerce crown.

It was a development hidden in plain sight. Shopee had made a claim in their earnings call, but we were the only media to dig deep into it.

In this story, we examined the claim. We spoke to various parties and looked at data – both public and private. We sketched out the reasons behind the rise. We listed the caveats – Shopee is still highly unprofitable.

The hours of work that stretched over two weeks paid off. There were critics, of course: Many people, especially in Singapore, couldn’t believe the claim. But it’s our job to burst bubbles: Singapore does not speak for the rest of the region.
