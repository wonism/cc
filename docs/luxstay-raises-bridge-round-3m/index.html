In brief: Vietnamese home-sharing startup nabs $3m in bridge round

Category : Brief, News
Date : 2019-01-02T08:10:17

Home-sharing startup Luxstay announced today that it has raised US$3 million in a bridge round from several investors including Tokyo-based CyberAgent Ventures (CAV), Y1 Ventures, and others. This is the second time CAV has invested in the startup. Founded in 2016, the Vietnam-based startup claims to have a network of 10,000 accommodations across Vietnam.
